Pendahuluan

The hr tag defines a thematic break in an HTML page (e.g. a shift of topic). The hr element is used to separate content (or define a change) in an HTML page.

Contoh koding:

<h1>Judul</h1> <hr width="50%" align="left" size="10"> <img src="image.png" width="500"> <hr> <p> Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
